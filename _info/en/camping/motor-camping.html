--- title: Motor Camping lang: en ref: motor-camping category: camping --- Those who come by motorbike have 2 options:
  • You buy a ticket for the Motor Camping which entitles you to place 1 motorbike + 1 small tent at the Camper Camping next to the festival terrain.. Cost: € 25, only accessible for motorbikers. See Camper Camping for further information.
  • You park your motorbike at Kortrijk XPO parking lot for €2 per day, take the free shuttle to the festival terrain and stay at the Tent Camping (€16 per person).
